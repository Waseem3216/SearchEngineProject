    About XYMZ

    Search Engine & Web Services for the whole wide web.

    Welcome to XYMZ, a retro-inspired search portal built to help people search anything and everything from one simple page.

    Company Background

    XYMZ is a classic early-2000s style web company with a modern mission: make online discovery fast, friendly, and fun. It blends the familiar search box of early web engines with the directory, news, tools, and community features of vintage internet portals.

    The company is presented as a Search Engine & Web Services brand offering web search, category browsing, custom portals, site listings, promotional placements, and discovery tools for individuals, schools, clubs, and small businesses.

    What XYMZ Does

    • Searches Web, Images, News, Videos, Wikipedia, and Maps.
    • Shows results inside a retro XYMZ Results window.
    • Supports saved searches, recent searches, and site submissions.
    • Includes fun portal tools like AnswerBot, Web Buddy, and Guestbook.

    Our Mission

    XYMZ helps users jump from curiosity to answers. The goal is to keep searching simple enough for anyone while making the internet feel exciting, colorful, and explorable again.

    Search Philosophy

    XYMZ combines old-school web-directory organization with live search shortcuts. It does not hide the web behind a plain screen; it gives users panels, tools, categories, saved links, and direct routes to information.

    Web Services

    XYMZ represents services like business directory listings, school and club portals, sponsored links, affiliate campaigns, local pages, and search-powered landing pages.

    XYMZ Help Center

    Tips, tricks, and support for better searching.

    How to Search

    1. Type a question, keyword, place, person, product, or idea into the search bar.
    2. Choose Web, Images, News, Videos, Wikipedia, or Maps.
    3. Click Search! and XYMZ will build an in-page results window with helpful destinations.

    Search Tips

    • Use specific words, such as “best soccer highlights.”
    • Use quotes for exact phrases.
    • Try fewer words if results feel too narrow.
    • Use directory links when you want ideas.

    Search Modes

    Web gives general search links. Images finds picture-search pages. News finds current coverage. Videos finds video platforms. Wikipedia can show encyclopedia summaries. Maps opens map search options.

    Fun Tools

    Use AnswerBot for quick project-friendly answers, Spin Web Wheel for random discovery, Save Current Search to bookmark terms, and Guestbook to leave messages stored in your browser.

    Browser Storage

    Recent searches, saved searches, guestbook entries, visitor count, theme, and submitted sites are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. They stay on your computer and can be cleared from your browser settings.
